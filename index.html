Matchmaking and Relationship Compatibility

This involves comparing the birth charts of two individuals to assess their compatibility. Astrologers look for harmonious or challenging aspects between the planets in each chart. It's used to gain insights into potential relationship dynamics, strengths, and weaknesses.

Forecasting and Timing

We use transits (the current movements of planets) and progressions (symbolic movements of planets) to forecast potential events and periods of change in a person's life.
